Mission

Our mission is to provide accurate and timely information related to government schemes to the citizens of our country.Our goal is to simplify the process of finding & understanding the details the details of government schemes so that citizens can easily access the benifits they are entitled to.

vision

Our Vision is to create a platform that serves as a one-stop-shop for information related to government schemes.We envision a future where all citizens have equal access to government resources and are able to take the advantage of the opportunities available to them.Our platform will be a hub for collaboration between citizens and government agencies,facilitating communication and feedback.
